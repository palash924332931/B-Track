B-Track Work Flow

B-Track is a complete solution for BRTC to track buses details information with different types of accounting report. There are few steps to track all buses activity and accounting details. It can easily track below activities:

  • Can track bus details which are on repair, which are on route.
  • Can easily track income of individual bus. It's may have on route income or different route income.
  • You can easily compare cost vs income of every bus.
  • Easily can generate different types of report.

Reports are:

  • Daily Bus Log Report
  • Daily Bus Position Report
  • Daily Income Report
  • Payslip Report which are used or not
  • Monthly Individual Bus Report Route Wise
  • Monthly Income Report Route Wise
  • Monthly Income Report Bus Type Wise

Modules are:

  • Bus history tracking
  • Accounting
  • Store Management
  • Reporting
